<a href="" id="how-to-add-a-file-type-association"></a>[如何新增檔案類型關聯](how-to-add-a-file-type-association.md)

<a href="" id="how-to-delete-a-file-type-association"></a>[如何刪除檔案類型關聯](how-to-delete-a-file-type-association.md)
